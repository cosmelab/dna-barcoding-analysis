Understanding Your Results

What is BLAST?

BLAST (Basic Local Alignment Search Tool) compares your DNA sequence against millions of sequences in GenBank to find the closest matches.

What does % identity mean?

  • 99-100%: Almost certainly the same species
  • 97-99%: Same species (standard DNA barcoding threshold)
  • 95-97%: Likely same species, possibly subspecies variation
  • 90-95%: Closely related species
  • <90%: Different species, possibly same genus

Why do all hits have the same % identity?

This is normal! Multiple database entries for the same species often have identical or nearly identical COI sequences, resulting in the same percent identity.

What if my top match isn't a mosquito?

This could indicate:

  • Contamination in your sample
  • Poor sequence quality leading to incorrect matches
  • A novel or undescribed species

Review your chromatograms and quality scores if results seem unexpected.
